# Historical Motor Oil Discussions — Annotated Links

Curated list of forum discussions on historical motor oils, early synthetics, base oil chemistry, and additive technologies.  
Each entry consists of a direct URL followed by a concise English description.

---

https://oil-glup.ru/threads/eon-e-11-polnaja-diehsterovaja-sintetika-iz-1974-goda-10w-40-variant-2.414/  
EON E-11 (1974, 10W-40, full diester synthetic PCMO by Pacer Petroleum/EON). Pioneer of 1970s synthetic revolution alongside All Proof and Amzoil. VOA analyses (KV, CCS) in PDF attachment.

https://oil-glup.ru/threads/mobil-shc-bez-ukazanija-vjazkosti-sovremennyj-standart-10w-60-pervaja-v-mire-pao-sintetika-1973-goda.20/page-3  
Mobil SHC (1973, no SAE grade, equivalent ~10W-60, first PAO + ester synthetic PCMO). VOA: KV100 25.81 cSt, VI 203, TBN 5.82, pour point -51°C. Historical predecessor to modern synthetics; PDF analysis.

https://oil-glup.ru/threads/mobil-1-5w-20-sovremennaja-0w-16-pervaja-v-mire-globalnaja-pao-sintetika-1975-goda.29/page-3  
Mobil 1 5W-20 (1975, actually ~0W-16, first global PAO + ester synthetic PCMO). VOA: KV100 6.96 cSt, VI 144, TBN 6.04, pour point <-60°C. Test sales 1975; debated fuel economy claims vs GM/Consumer Reports. PDF included.

https://oil-glup.ru/threads/freedom-sae-10w-40-api-se-100-diehsterovaja-sintetika-s-simvolicheskim-nazvaniem-iz-1978.352/page-2  
Freedom SAE 10W-40 API SE (1978, 100% diester synthetic >70% ester, by Cord Oil/Amway-linked). VOA: KV100 14.35 cSt, VI 185, TBN 8.71, pour point -51°C. Rare MLM-era oil; PDF analysis.

https://oil-glup.ru/threads/all-proof-synthetic-sae-10w-40-pervoe-100-ehster-pcmo-samoe-nachalo-1970-x.334/page-2  
All Proof Synthetic SAE 10W-40 (early 1970s, first 100% ester PCMO by Chemical Research Labs, Stabl Flo additives). VOA: KV100 15.90 cSt, VI 213, TBN 1.6, pour point <-60°C. Predates Amsoil; PDF analysis.

https://oil-glup.ru/threads/xonex-sl-1000-10w-40-100-diehsterovaja-sintetika-serediny-1970-x.289/page-2  
Xonex SL-1000 (mid-1970s, 10W-40, 100% diester synthetic PCMO). VOA: KV100 14.74 cSt, VI 191, TBN 8.22, pour point -54°C. Exxon lawsuit over name (later Zenex); PDF analysis.

https://oil-glup.ru/threads/amsoil-avoil-sae-15w-50-polnaja-aviacionno-porshnevaja-bezzolnaja-pao-sintetika-iz-1983.26/page-2  
Amsoil AVOIL SAE 15W-50 (1983, ashless PAO+ester aviation piston oil). VOA: KV100 19.49 cSt, VI 182, TBN 0.5, pour point -53°C. Inspired by Aeroshell; ester-rich. PDF analysis.

https://oil-glup.ru/threads/sinclair-dino-supreme-unikalnoe-maslo-10w-40-s-nikel-fosfornoj-prisadkoj-serediny-1960-x.27/  
Sinclair Dino Supreme (mid-1960s, 10W-40 mineral with nickel-phosphorus additive). VOA: KV100 16.24 cSt, VI 211, TBN 8.78, pour point -37°C. Patented Ni for wear protection. PDF analysis.

https://oil-glup.ru/threads/amzoil-super-premium-100-diehsterovaja-sintetika-sae-10w-40-pervogo-pokolenija-1973.331/  
Amzoil Super Premium SAE 10W-40 (1973, 100% diester synthetic PCMO). VOA: KV100 14.47 cSt, VI 198, TBN 8.85, pour point -54°C. First Amsoil; disputed "world's first" claim. PDF VOA.

https://oil-glup.ru/threads/anderol-471-ashless-ester-sinteticheskoe-bezzolnoe-maslo-c-tcp-dlja-porshnevoj-aviacii-1960-g.31/  
Anderol 471 (1960s, ~5W-40, 100% ester ashless aviation oil with TCP). VOA: KV100 15.17 cSt, VI 199, TBN 0.26, pour point -52°C. First full ester AVOIL; influenced PCMO synthetics. PDF analysis.

https://oil-glup.ru/threads/amoco-ultimate-5w-30-pao-maslo-1980-x-davshee-nazvanie-toplivu-bp-i-aral-ultimate.346/  
Amoco Ultimate 5W-30 (1980s, PAO synthetic PCMO). VOA: KV100 10.27 cSt, VI 158, TBN 7.3, pour point -42°C. Early US PAO oil; name influenced BP/Aral fuels. PDF VOA.

https://oil-glup.ru/threads/prestone-pervaja-v-mire-tovarnaja-pcmo-pvl-sintetika-10w-10-iz-1950-x-sintezirovana-v-1943.19/page-3  
Prestone (1950s, 10W-10, first commercial PCMO PAG synthetic, synthesized 1943). VOA: KV100 6.24 cSt, VI 148, TBN 0.2, pour point -51°C. Non-petroleum, engine-cleaning. PDF analysis.

https://oil-glup.ru/threads/indeks-vjazkosti.380/  
Viscosity Index thread listing historic oils: Ultron 10W-40 (diester synthetic, VI 230), SOHIO Nitrex 5W-40 (mineral, VI 223), All Proof 5W-40 (ester, VI 213), Mobil SHC (PAO+ester, VI 203). Comparative overview.

https://oil-glup.ru/threads/macmillan-ring-free-motor-oil-sae-20-unikalnoe-naftenovoe-mineralnoe-maslo-1940-goda.309/  
Macmillan Ring-Free Motor Oil SAE 20 (1940s): unique naphthenic mineral base from Macmillan Petroleum. Patented process removes carbon deposits without additives. Historical ads claim fuel savings and cleaner engines; VOA shows no additives or wear metals.

https://oil-glup.ru/threads/mobil-mobiloil-sae-30-klassicheskaja-mineralka-1960-x.9/  
Mobil Mobiloil SAE 30 (1960s): classic mineral PCMO by Socony Mobil. High phosphorus and barium from phosphonate additives. Historical racing use; VOA PDF attached.

https://oil-glup.ru/threads/agip-f-1-supermotoroil-20w-50-redkaja-gonochnaja-evropejskaja-polusintetika-nachala-1970-x.270/  
AGIP F.1 Supermotoroil 20W-50 (early 1970s): European semi-synthetic PCMO. High VI, strong additive system, low wear and oxidation. Data sheet available.

https://oil-glup.ru/threads/amsoil-super-premium-100-diehsterovaja-sintetika-sae-10w-40-vtorogo-pokolenija-1974.319/  
Amsoil Super Premium SAE 10W-40 (1974–1978, second generation). 100% diester synthetic with extended drain claims. High film strength and detergency; VOA shows low wear.

https://oil-glup.ru/threads/aral-kowal-20w-20-legendarnyj-analog-m8v-iz-1970-x.14/  
Aral Kowal 20W-20 (1970s): mineral HDEO, analog of Soviet M8V. Moderate ash additives; data sheet attached.

https://oil-glup.ru/threads/convoy-10w-50-parasintetika-1977-goda.116/  
Convoy 10W-50 parasynthetic PCMO (1977/1980s) by Conklin. Moly-based additives, extended drain claims, high VI and TBN. Analysis PDF.

Love Synthetic Lubricant SAE 20W-50 (1976–1977): highly pure PAO synthetic with MoS₂ suspension. Minor ester for dispersion; low wear. Data sheet attached.

https://oil-glup.ru/threads/prestone-pervaja-v-mire-tovarnaja-pcmo-pvl-sintetika-10w-10-iz-1950-x-sintezirovana-v-1943.19/page-2  
Prestone synthetic 10W-10 (1950s, synthesized 1943): first commercial PAG-based PCMO. High VI, excellent cold flow, limited market adoption. VOA PDF.

Valvoline Chemaloy HD Detergent SAE 20W-20 (1960s): mineral oil with boronated dispersants. Emphasis on cleaning and wear protection; data sheet attached.

https://oil-glup.ru/threads/all-proof-synthetic-sae-10w-50-pervoe-100-ehster-pcmo-1974-goda.32/  
All Proof Synthetic SAE 10W-50 (1974): first 100% ester PCMO (ENOCA). High VI, extreme additive levels, very low TBN. Linked to early Red Line lineage.
